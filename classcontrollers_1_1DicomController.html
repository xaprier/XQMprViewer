Detailed Description

Scans a directory for DICOM series and loads them asynchronously.

Call LoadDicom() to start a background scan; connect SeriesListReady to learn the number of series found. Call LoadSeries() to read a specific series and connect ImageDataReady to receive the resulting vtkImageData.
