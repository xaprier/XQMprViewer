Detailed Description

Shared control panel used by both Viewport and Multi-Window modes.

Stacks a ControllerPanelDicomItem (folder/series selection), a ControllerPanelSphereItem (sphere toggle, radius, colour), a ControllerPanelFPSOverlayItem (FPS overlay configuration), and a ControllerPanelOrientationMarkerItem (orientation marker configuration) with separators.
