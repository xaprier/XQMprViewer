Detailed Description

Normalised coordinate and display settings for a single viewport.

All coordinates follow VTK convention: normalised [0.0, 1.0] range with the origin at the bottom-left corner.

qvv::ViewportConfig axial;
axial.xMin = 0.0; axial.xMax = 0.33;
axial.label = "Axial";
